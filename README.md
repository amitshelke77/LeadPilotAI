# LeadPilot AI

LeadPilot AI is an AI-powered business automation platform designed to automate lead generation, company research, website scraping, email extraction, and AI-assisted business workflows.

## Vision

Build a reusable automation platform that helps businesses save time on repetitive research tasks.

## Planned Modules

- Company Search
- Website Discovery
- Website Scraper
- Email Extractor
- Phone Number Extractor
- Excel Export
- AI Company Summary
- AI Cold Email Generator
- Workflow Automation

## Technology Stack

- Python
- Streamlit
- FastAPI
- Playwright
- BeautifulSoup
- SQLite
- Pandas
- Gemini AI
- OpenRouter
